UX practice: Evaluation of usability

With a focus on "SVT Play" mobile application

  • Course: UX process 3: Evaluation of Usability
  • Task: To evaluate usability för "SVT Play" mobile application
  • Period: 22 Feb - 28 Mar 2021 (5 weeks)
  • Team: Andreas Engström, Helena Dahlberg, Julia Chae, Tara O'Keefe, Ulrika Roslund Gidlöf (Nackademin UX 20)
  • Methods: Heuristisk utvärdering, kartläggning av behov, cognitive walkthrough, competitive benchmarking, Usability test, Survey about attitude, Preferenstest (A/B), analysis, Trianglurate method
  • Tools: Miro, google forms (Survey), Ends canvas (offboarding)
