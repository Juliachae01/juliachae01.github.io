UX-project: Caspeco "Mitt Schema"

Internship 2021

Caspeco is a fast-growing company, located in Uppsala that offers operating systems including salary, booking, schedule, cash, payment, analysis for the restaurant industry. Today, they deliver cloud-based digital solutions, both in desktop and mobile applications for restaurateurs and their employees.

To evaluate and redesign Caspeco's "Mitt Schema" product

During my internship period, I worked mainly with evaluating and redesigning Caspeco's "Mitt schema" ("My schedule" in English) product.

There are two different types of schedules:
1. "Stora schema" - Users can create and add schedules for staffs, it is used by the restaurant managers and administrators who have the power to manage staff.

2. "Mitt schema" - Users can check their work schedule, manage their available working hours and apply for absence.

"Mitt schema" is used by about 12,000 users while the Stora schema is used by 1,000 people.
The problem was that "Mitt schema" has not been developed for so many years because Caspeco's close customers are restaurant managers who do not usually use the mobile app, so they focused more on the "Stora Schema".

Therefore, my task was to explore user problems, find out their pain points and sketch design solutions.

Design thinking iteration process

Image: work process


I iterated the Design thinking process around 4 times.
I empathized with users' problems in different ways and sketched ideas and defined MVP and ended up the Lo-fi wireframes, tested it with customers.
Then I identified new problems and sketched again.
